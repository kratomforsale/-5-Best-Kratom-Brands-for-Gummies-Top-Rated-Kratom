## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are kratom gummies legal?

Kratom is legal at the federal level in the United States, but **banned or restricted in several states** including Alabama, Arkansas, Indiana, Rhode Island, Vermont, and Wisconsin. Always check your local laws before purchasing. (Source: American Kratom Association, State Legality Map, 2023)

### How long do kratom gummies take to kick in?

Most users report effects between **20–45 minutes** after consumption. Gummies take slightly longer than powder due to digestion but typically longer than kratom capsules in some users' experience.

### What's the difference between kratom extract gummies and regular kratom gummies?

Regular gummies contain plain leaf powder or ground kratom. Extract gummies are concentrated — significantly higher mitragynine per serving. Extract gummies are more potent and require lower doses.

### Can I take kratom gummies every day?

Daily use is a personal decision that warrants consultation with a healthcare professional. Regular kratom users report building tolerance over time. Many experienced users recommend rotating strains and taking periodic breaks.

### How do I verify a brand's lab testing?

Look for a Certificate of Analysis (CoA) from an **ISO-accredited third-party laboratory**. The CoA should include the batch number, test date, and specific results for alkaloids, heavy metals, and microbials. Jack Botanicals publishes all CoAs publicly on their website.
